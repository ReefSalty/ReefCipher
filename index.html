Reef Cipher Encrypter/Decrypter

How to Use the Reef Cipher Tool

  1. Enter the text you want to encrypt or decrypt in the first box.
  2. To encrypt, leave the key box empty. To decrypt, enter the key in the key box.
  3. Click "Encrypt" to encrypt your text or "Decrypt" to decrypt it.
  4. The result will appear in the output area below.
  5. Use the "Export as Text File" button to download the result and key.
